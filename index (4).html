§ 02 — What it does

One agent that owns the whole booking conversation.

Every "can I book Thursday?", "can I move my appointment?", and "actually, cancel that" gets handled by the agent — not left on your desk or missed after hours.

A.01

Books in the conversation

Reads your live availability, books the right service for the right duration, and writes it straight into your calendar — no double-bookings, no manual checks.

A.02

Reschedules & cancels

Finds the booking by email — no IDs to remember — moves it in one turn, or cancels with the reason written onto the event so you always know why a slot opened up.

A.03

Never misses, never sleeps

Answers instantly at 2am, on weekends, and through your busiest rush — so the customers you'd otherwise miss become appointments instead of voicemails.

A.04

Routes to the right person

"I want Sarah for a balayage" goes to Sarah; "whoever's free" checks the whole team. Each staff member's services, prices, and availability stay accurate.

A.05

Speaks your customers' language

Handles chat and voice in 30+ languages, confirms times in the caller's own timezone, and sounds warm and human — never robotic.

A.06

Built around your brand

Tone, styling, and personality tuned to match your business — the serene, premium feel your customers already expect, carried into every reply.

§ 03 — How it works

Four steps. One short onboarding.

No Zapier, no glue code, and no prompt-engineering on your end. We do the wiring; you approve the result.

01

Connect your calendar

One secure sign-in lets the agent read availability and write appointments — to the calendars you choose, revocable any time.

02

We train it on you

We load your services, durations, prices, and team into the agent and tune its voice to sound exactly like your business.

03

Pick your channels

Website chat, phone line, WhatsApp — or all of them. Same agent, same brain, every surface in sync.

04

Go live

Test a few bookings yourself, then point your customers at it. Most clients are live within days, not months.
